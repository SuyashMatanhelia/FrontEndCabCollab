About Us

Hi There! We are a 4 member team at CabCollab. CabCollab will be a mobile application that will allow the registered users to carpool with people from the same gated communities and offices. A user will be able to use this app if he has his own vehicle or if he wants to share a cab with someone. Our app eliminates the safety concerns that come to mind when talking about carpooling, since people only from the same community are able to pool the cab together. This is because the app will allow communities to register their trusted members on the app who can put up their own rides on the app whenever they are going from or to that place. This will especially benefit corporate offices, societies and other communities where people trust each other and would like a carpooling option to save money.
